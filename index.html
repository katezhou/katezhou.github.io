Welcome! My name is Kaitlyn Zhou (she/her) and I'm a Ph.D. student at Stanford University. I am advised by Professor Dan Jurafsky. My recent work has focused on the analysis of language models, the fairness implications of NLG evaluations, and the language of online mis- and disinformation. I have previously interned at Microsoft Research FATE where I worked with Alexandra Olteanu and Su Lin Blodgett. I completed my Bachelor's and Master's at the University of Washington and worked with Kate Starbird and Emma Spiro.
