Welcome! My name is Kaitlyn Zhou (she/her) and I'm a Ph.D. student at Stanford University. I am advised by Professor Dan Jurafsky and work at the intersection of artificial intelligence and human-computer interactions. I completed my Bachelor's and Master's at the University of Washington and worked with Kate Starbird and Emma Spiro. I'm most excited about designing and researching algorithms that are beyond being fair, algorithms that push us to diversify our thinking, help us understand our own biases, and uplift underrepresented voices.

PERSONAL
